About Us

If you're a busy student on the run, it can be difficult to find meals that are quick to make and satisfying to eat. Nutrition is a core requirement of success, but how can you maintain diet goals when you have very little time to research, shop, and cook? Yeti Eats to the rescue! We have done all the hard work for you. We provide easy-to-prepare recipes that are nutritionally desirable, require no hard-to-get ingredients, and utilize no special appliances. Plus, we provide a calorie tracker so you can choose your maintenance goals and stick to your daily plan. Not to mention the message board that allows you to socialize with other amateur chefs and swap recipes. Sign up today, and enjoy all these great benefits!

  • Each recipe takes 15 minutes or less. Save time and enjoy!
  • All recipes have a minimal ingredient list. Some have 5 or less!
  • No special equipment is required. Check out our one-pot wonders to save time on clean-up!
  • Nutrition is at the heart of each recipe. Feel good about what you eat!
  • For added success, use the calorie calculator to track daily increments!
